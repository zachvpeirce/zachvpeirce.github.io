Welcome

Hello! Welcome to my online profile/resume/portfolio extravaganza. My friends, colleagues, and mentors have all suggested making a website about myself and finally I did it. It took six years to break from building things for others to make a page of my own, but I finally did it. I would like to stress the amount of time I spent thinking about what would go into this biography.

I was born on a dark and stormy night a long, long time ago. I grew up quickly and moved 11 times. Here is a quick summary:

  • I once dreamt of being a trash man.
  • I have visited every state in the continental US.
  • I won a gold medal at gymnastic nationals on the trampoline.
  • I was a huge activist for the Darfur Anti-Genocide Movement.
  • I have been a waiter for 7 years.
  • I like to break things and fix them again.

As a college graduate I am apparently supposed to be serious so that I can "get a job" and "raise a family." Even more exciting, I am told I should "get into a good graduate program." However, my goals differ from the mainstream.
